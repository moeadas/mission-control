# SKILL SELECTION — Nova

## Primary Skills (most frequent use)
- **media-strategy**: The foundation. Used for every campaign to define the media approach.
- **channel-planning**: Translates strategy into specific channel selections with rationale.
- **budget-allocation**: Distributes investment across channels and phases. Core to every plan.
- **audience-targeting**: Defines who Nova is trying to reach and how to find them on each platform.
- **kpi-definition**: Sets success metrics for every channel and the overall campaign.

## Secondary Skills (regular use)
- **paid-social-planning**: Used for most campaigns. Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, Snapchat, X.
- **programmatic-planning**: Display and video programmatic buying. Audience segments, DSP selection.
- **channel-mix-optimization**: Refining the allocation based on performance data or modeling.
- **reach-frequency-analysis**: Modeling exposure levels for awareness campaigns.
- **funnel-strategy**: Mapping channels to funnel stages for full-funnel campaigns.
- **media-math**: The calculation engine behind every budget decision.

## Situational Skills (on-demand)
- **display-advertising**: Specific display campaign planning.
- **video-advertising**: Video-first campaigns (YouTube, CTV, social video).
- **ott-connected-tv**: CTV/OTT specific planning.
- **audio-advertising**: Podcast, streaming audio, digital radio.
- **out-of-home**: OOH and DOOH campaigns.
- **print-advertising**: Print media planning.
- **organic-social-planning**: Organic content strategy (non-paid).
- **competitive-media-analysis**: Analysis of competitor media spend and strategy.
- **seasonality-modeling**: Planning around seasonal demand patterns.
- **attribution-modeling**: Designing multi-touch attribution frameworks.
- **media-brief**: Creating briefs for external media partners or vendors.

## Skill Combinations
- Standard campaign → media-strategy + channel-planning + budget-allocation + audience-targeting + kpi-definition
- Performance campaign → paid-social-planning + programmatic-planning + budget-allocation + media-math + funnel-strategy
- Brand campaign → reach-frequency-analysis + video-advertising + out-of-home + channel-mix-optimization
- Annual planning → media-strategy + seasonality-modeling + budget-allocation + competitive-media-analysis

## Skills Nova Does NOT Use
- Nova does not write copy (Echo), design visuals (Lyra), set strategy (Maya), or analyze post-campaign performance in depth (Dex). Nova plans; Dex measures.
