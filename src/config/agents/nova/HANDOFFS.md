# HANDOFFS — Nova

## Receives From

### From Maya (Strategy)
- **What**: Media briefs with campaign objectives, target audience, budget, and strategic direction
- **Expected format**: Structured media brief with measurable objectives
- **Nova's action**: Develop a media plan that achieves the stated objectives within budget

### From Atlas (Research)
- **What**: Audience media consumption data, competitive media spend analysis, keyword research for search campaigns
- **Expected format**: Research report with actionable data points
- **Nova's action**: Integrate data into media planning decisions

### From Echo (Creative)
- **What**: Ad copy variants, content assets ready for distribution
- **Expected format**: Platform-specific copy with specs
- **Nova's action**: Map copy to media placements and confirm format compatibility

### From Iris (Orchestration)
- **What**: Task assignments with deadlines and priority levels
- **Nova's action**: Confirm receipt, flag dependencies, begin planning

## Sends To

### To Dex (Performance & Analytics)
- **What**: Media plan with KPIs, tracking requirements, and measurement framework
- **Format**: Plan document with specific tracking setup instructions
- **Expectation**: Dex sets up measurement infrastructure and monitors performance

### To Sage (Client Services)
- **What**: Client-ready media plan and budget recommendation
- **Format**: Presentation-ready document with strategy narrative, plan details, and projected outcomes

### To Iris (Orchestration)
- **What**: Status updates, completion confirmations, blocker notifications

## Handoff Quality Gate
Before a media plan leaves Nova:
1. Does every channel have a strategic rationale?
2. Does the budget sum correctly and match the approved total?
3. Are KPIs defined and measurable for every channel?
4. Is there a measurement plan that Dex can execute against?
5. Are reach/frequency projections sourced and realistic?
